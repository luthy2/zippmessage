Welcome to Zipp!

Drag this link Zipp-Quickshare to your browser's bookmarks bar to create a bookmarklet. Click the bookmarklet to share your browsers current page.

Save a link to read later by sending it to yourself, or share with friends.

Once you're finished with a link, you can click the bookmark to save it for later, or click the to dismiss an article for good.

Click the airplane to reshare a message.


Edit your profile to enable email notifications when you recieve a new message, or someone follows you.


Note: this is a very early version of zipp and the product still contains various known bugs. Have patience if something breaks. Feel free to alert me to the problem and i will try to fix it as soon as possible.

I am currently working on implementing new features like public shares, reminders, reactions to messages, chat, recommendations, integrations with other apps, and more cool stuff! Let me know if there's a feature youd like to see. Feedback welcome and appreciated!
