Welcome to Zipp!

Save a link to read later by sending it to yourself, or share with friends.

Once you're finished with a link, you can click the bookmark to save it for later, or click the X to dismiss an article for good.

Click the airplane to reshare a message!


Edit your profile to enable email notifications when you recieve a new message, or someone follows you.

Drag this link Zipp-Quickshare to your browser's bookmarks bar to create a bookmarklet. Click the bookmarklet to easily share your browsers current page.


Note: this is a very early version of zipp and the product still contains various known bugs. Have patience if something breaks, and feel free to alert me to the problem.

I am currently working on implementing new features like public shares, reminders, reactions to messages, chat, recommendations, integrations with other apps, and more cool stuff! feedback welcome and appreciated!
